## Venture Captal & Angel Investors

### Climate change specific
* [Huge list of greentech VC firms](http://www.boogar.com/resources/venturecapital/clean_tech.htm)
* [88 Green Ventures](http://www.88greenventures.com/index.html): early stage investment for commercialization of green technology. Global, based in Australia
* [LOHAS Capital](http://lohascapital.com/): $50mil max equity crowdfunding for cleantech and sustainability companies - relatively full-service help to raise the round
* [Ramez Naam's clean energy AngelList syndicate](https://angel.co/ramez-1/syndicate)
* [PRIME](http://primecoalition.org/): a nonprofit focused on connecting philanthropic investment with companies combating climate change. They have two programs: (1) curate a shortlist of companies for investors and (2) educate the public on this idea
